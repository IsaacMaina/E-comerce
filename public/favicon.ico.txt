To add a favicon to your application, place a favicon.ico file in the public directory.

You can generate a favicon at sites like:
- https://www.favicon-generator.org/
- https://realfavicongenerator.net/
- https://favicon.io/

Make sure your favicon is:
- 16x16, 32x32, or 48x48 pixels
- In .ico format
- Named favicon.ico
